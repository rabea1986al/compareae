Affiliate Disclosure

Full transparency on how CompareAE earns revenue — and why it never costs you anything extra.

Our Commitment to Transparency

✅ CompareAE earns affiliate commissions from certified digital aggregators and insurance partners (such as YallaCompare) at absolutely zero cost to the user. When you click a partner link on our platform and complete a purchase, we may receive a commission from that partner. You pay exactly the same price whether you come through our site or visit the partner directly.

How Affiliate Links Work

When you click a "See Your Price" or similar call-to-action button on CompareAE, you are directed to a third-party insurance platform. These links contain a unique tracking identifier that allows our affiliate partner to attribute your visit to CompareAE.

If you complete a purchase on that platform, we receive a commission. This is how we fund our platform and keep our comparison tools free for all users.

  • All affiliate links are clearly labelled with rel="sponsored" or rel="nofollow" attributes in compliance with Google's guidelines.
  • Our editorial decisions — which companies to feature, how to rank comparisons, and what content to publish — are not influenced by affiliate relationships.
  • We do not accept payment for editorial placement or favourable reviews.

Our Partners

CompareAE works with certified digital aggregators and licensed insurance platforms operating in the UAE, which may include but are not limited to:

  • YallaCompare
  • Policybazaar UAE
  • Other licensed UAE insurance platforms

All partners are selected based on their regulatory compliance, product range, and user experience — not commission rates.

Pricing Independence

CompareAE does not set, negotiate, or influence the insurance prices displayed or available on partner platforms. All pricing is determined solely by the insurer or aggregator based on your personal underwriting data.

Questions?

If you have questions about our affiliate relationships or editorial independence, contact us at info@compareae.com.

CompareAE is an independent informational and comparison platform. We do not issue insurance policies, act as licensed insurance brokers, or provide financial advice. Insurance products, pricing, approvals, and coverage terms are handled directly by insurers or licensed partners.
