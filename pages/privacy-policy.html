Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who We Are

CompareAE ("we", "us", "our") operates compareae.com, an independent car insurance comparison platform serving the United Arab Emirates. We are not an insurance company, broker, or financial institution.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate our platform:

  • Usage Data: Page views, time on page, and anonymous click behavior collected via server logs.
  • Affiliate Clicks: When you click a partner link, your click is tracked anonymously for commission attribution purposes. No personal data is transmitted by us.
  • Contact Form: If you contact us via email, we retain your email address solely to respond to your enquiry.

We do not collect names, phone numbers, Emirates ID details, or financial information.

3. Cookies

CompareAE uses only essential functional cookies required for site operation. We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or third-party tracking beyond affiliate link attribution.

4. Third-Party Links

Our site contains links to third-party insurance platforms (such as YallaCompare). When you follow these links, you are subject to those platforms' privacy policies, which we do not control. We recommend reviewing their policies before submitting personal information.

5. Data Retention

Anonymous server logs are retained for a maximum of 90 days. Email correspondence is retained for 12 months unless you request deletion earlier.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@compareae.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of CompareAE after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

CompareAE is an independent informational and comparison platform. We do not issue insurance policies, act as licensed insurance brokers, or provide financial advice. Insurance products, pricing, approvals, and coverage terms are handled directly by insurers or licensed partners.
