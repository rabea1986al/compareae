Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Comprehensive and TPL?

Comprehensive covers your car and the other party. TPL covers only the other party and is cheaper.

Are the prices shown accurate?

Prices are estimates. The actual price depends on your car year, age, and driving history.

Is GCC cover important?

Yes if you travel to GCC countries. Make sure your policy includes it.

How long does it take to get insured?

Less than 10 minutes online. Policy is emailed immediately after payment.
