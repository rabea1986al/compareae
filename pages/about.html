About CompareAE

An independent platform helping UAE residents and expats compare car insurance — clearly, honestly, and for free.

Who We Are

CompareAE is an independent informational and comparison platform focused on car insurance in the United Arab Emirates. We were built to solve a simple problem: finding the right car insurance in the UAE is confusing, time-consuming, and often opaque.

We are not an insurance company, broker, or agent. We do not issue policies or handle claims. Our role is to present structured, factual comparisons that help you make your own informed decision.

Our Mission

To provide UAE residents — nationals, expats, new drivers, and long-time residents — with clear, unbiased, and accessible information about car insurance options across all seven emirates.

  • Comprehensive vs Third-Party (TPL) comparisons
  • Agency Repair vs Non-Agency Repair guidance
  • GCC extension coverage explanations
  • Young driver and new resident insurance guidance
  • EV and luxury car insurance context

Editorial Standards

All content on CompareAE is reviewed for factual consistency with UAE insurance regulations, insurer policy wordings, and RTA guidelines. We do not publish speculative pricing or unverified claims. For more details, see our Editorial Policy.

How We Generate Revenue

CompareAE earns affiliate commissions when users click through to our certified partners (such as YallaCompare) and complete a purchase. This comes at absolutely zero additional cost to you. For full details, see our Affiliate Disclosure.

CompareAE is an independent informational and comparison platform. We do not issue insurance policies, act as licensed insurance brokers, or provide financial advice. Insurance products, pricing, approvals, and coverage terms are handled directly by insurers or licensed partners.
